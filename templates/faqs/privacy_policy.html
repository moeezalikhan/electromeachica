At Electromechanica, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following information about you:

  • Personal Details

    • Your name, email address, phone number, company name, or any information you submit through inquiry or contact forms.

  • Technical Data

    • Your IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, and time spent on our website.

  • Usage Data

    • Details on how you interact with our website, pages visited, and features accessed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Process service requests and communicate project updates.
  • Improve our website performance, functionality, and user experience.
  • Send important service announcements or promotional communications (only with your consent).

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site performance. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but certain site features may not function properly without them.

4. Data Security

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.
While we strive to secure your information, no method of transmission is 100% risk-free.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We only share information with:

  • Trusted service providers who assist in operating our website or delivering services.
  • Legal authorities when required by applicable law.

All third-party partners adhere to strict confidentiality and data protection standards.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Object to or restrict certain data processing.
  • Request data portability.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at elecmech.pak@gmail.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. All updates will be posted on this page with a revised 1st January 2026 date.
